DOT DOT DOT MAGAZINE

Year: 2018
Together with: Alessandro Ferrari, Giulia Cortinovis, Stefano Pisana, Zhu Hanyu, Giacomo Bevacqua

During an editorial design class, together with the team, we rethought and redesigned Dot Dot Dot magazine, an independent publication of visual culture designed by Stuart Bailey and Peter Bil’ak. The boost that has come from the development of New Media has permitted the reapropriation of the past, and its recontextualization in new shapes. In this environment, visual and artistic culture is influenced by these new/old models; hybrid communication forms are created, suspended between past and present, a gateway to the future. This stream of innovation, that has its roots in past paradigms, is at the base of Dot Dot Dot’s return which, with its first issue “Revive”, beckons to think on this theme, aiming to present perspectives and debates on a lifestyle that absorbs our culture.

Dot Dot Dot radio is a transmedia extension of the magazine, hosted on dotdotdot.com. Accessible and democratic, the web radio was deemed as the right choice to spread Dot Dot Dot’s culture pills to a wider audience.
