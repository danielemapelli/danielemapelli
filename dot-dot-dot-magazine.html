DOT DOT DOT MAGAZINE

Year: 2018 / Together with Alessandro Ferrari, Giulia Cortinovis, Stefano Pisana, Zhu Hanyu, Giacomo Bevacqua

Dot Dot Dot was an independent magazine of visual culture produced by graphic designers Stuart Bailey and Peter Bil’ak. During an editorial design class, the magazine was given a new mission, a new structure and tone of voice. The new Dot Dot Dot explores open contemporary culture through different narrative styles and pieces of writing both from estabilished authors and open call participants.
The boost that has come from the development of New Media has permitted the reappropriation of the past, and its recontextualization in new shapes. In this environment, visual and artistic culture is influenced by these new/old models; hybrid communication forms are created, suspended between past and present, a gateway to the future. This stream of innovation, that has its roots in past paradigms, is at the base of Dot Dot Dot’s return which, with its first issue “Revive”, beckons to think on this theme, aiming to present perspectives and debates on a lifestyle that absorbs our culture. Dot Dot Dot Radio is a transmedia extension of the magazine. Accessible and democratic, the web radio was deemed as the right choice to spread Dot Dot Dot’s culture pills to a wider audience.
