DILLO

Year: 2019
Together with: Francesca Granzotto, Elena Stefani, Mattia Lombardini, Federica Campanelli, Anna Cravero

Created after preliminary user research, Dillo is the new inclusive sex education service that helps pre-teens becoming familiar and comfortable with all aspects of their sexuality. As suggested by its name (the imperative form of the Italian verb “dire”), Dillo encourages teens to share their innermost thoughts and to not keep everything inside. Provided to each class, the interactive smart object collects both personal and common doubts on sexuality issues. The smartphone app is a smart object’s digital extension that leads users to ask questions from more comfortable environments, such as their own bedroom. The digital extension hosts a quiz game that tests users knowledge about sexuality and affectivity topics. Doubts, questions, misconceptions are then categorised, collected and sent to a dedicated section of Dillo website. Here, datas can be consulted by educators in order to build specific sex education lesson plans.
