READ–ON

Year: 2020
Together with: Susanna Colombo, Yasmine Michielin, Stefano Pisana, Giulia Valentinuzzi

During the 21st century, time, cost and skills are not an obstacle anymore: everyone can easily learn how to fabricate convincing fake news. For these reasons, perhaps mis- and disinformation propagate across the web with growing frequency and sophistication. Read–on is a satirical project which aim is raising awareness amongst young adults of fake news and their main negative effect: hate speech. 00s pop visuals were deemed as the right choice to catch the attention of the target. So using pop culture icons and bright colours, together with the team, we designed Instagram feed (a serie of informative pills to increase awareness on the issue) and a microsite. Here, a randomiser makes the users play a pop-quiz on various social issues topics (that could change periodically).
