STEREOTIPI.MP4

Year: 2018
Together with: Elena Stefani, Renato Craveiro, Gianluca Rossi, Mariasole Parlato

As the result of visual analysis of the 100 most viewed pop and hip hop videoclips of the decade (2010-2018), Stereotipi.mp4 is an awareness campaign against unconscious sexualised gender stereotypes in music videoclips. What apparently seems just our favourite pop star poster is actually a deep message which aim is to inform and sensitize about the negative effects of stereotyped gender representations. As stated by American sociologist Stephen T. Russell, music and accompanying clip that reflect issues of gender through sexual-stereotyped themes often prime perceptions and influence the imitation of behaviors and attitudes viewed through the recall of schema.
